Sæhrímnir Stew

Sisyphus didn't feel so unlucky when he heard about this boar

This hearty stew is guaranteed to feed a heavenly hall of ghostly warriors nightly.

Ingredients

  • One mythical boar

Steps

  1. Step 1: Slaughter Sæhrímnir.
  2. Step 2: Throw him into your cauldron Eldhrímnir.
  3. Step 3: Add whatever you have lying around and simmer over low heat for 6-8 hours.
  4. Step 4: Serve hot!
