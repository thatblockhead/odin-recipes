Mead of Poetry

This traditional beverage is just the thing to quench your thirst after a hard day of berserking

Tired of being tongue-tied when reciting your poetry? Want to become a scholar without all the hard work? This mythical drink will do the trick. Whip up this concoction with just a few simple ingredients, and boundless eloquence can be yours. Just watch out for disguised gods who may try to steal it from you!

Ingredients
  • Raw unfiltered honey
  • Spring water (best if sourced from recently-conquered lands)
  • Kvasir's blood
Steps
  1. Step 1: Kill Kvasir and drain his blood.
  2. Step 2: Divide the blood between your pots Son and Bodn.
  3. Step 3: Stir blood, spring water, and honey in your kettle Odrerir over medium-low heat.
  4. Step 4: Remove mixture from heat and leave uncovered for 3-5 days to allow exposure to wild yeast.
  5. Step 5: Store in a cool, dark place in a covered but not airtight container (CO2 needs to escape) for 2-4 weeks, stirring every few days
  6. Step 6 Optional: Transfer to secondary covered but not airtight container and allow to age for anywhere from several weeks to several months.
  7. Step 7: Guard your mead closely from any suspicious wanderers.
